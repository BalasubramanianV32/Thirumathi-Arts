Common Questions

FAQ

❓
How long does mehendi last? +

With proper care, mehendi can last 10–15 days on hands and longer on feet. Keeping it moist while drying and avoiding water contact for the first few hours helps the colour darken beautifully.

How early should I book bridal mehendi? +

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for bridal mehendi, especially during wedding seasons (Oct–Feb). Send us a WhatsApp to check our availability early!

Is the henna paste natural and safe? +

Yes! We only use 100% natural henna paste with no harmful chemicals or black dye. It's safe for skin, including for pregnant women. We do not use black henna under any circumstances.

Do you travel outside the city? +

Yes, we do travel for weddings and events. A travel allowance is applicable depending on the distance. Contact us to discuss your event location and we'll make it work!
